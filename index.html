HeadLine

The purpose of this project is to analyze and visualize US population health data based on U.S. state specific Census data from the year 2014 using D3(Data Driven Document). Three factors Poverty, Age and Incomeare plotted aginst risk factors Lack of Healthcare, Obesity and Smoking percentages.

US Sate Population Health Risks and Age, Income

The above scatter plot when observed for US State' Poverty to Healthcare, revealed that Texas state population needs more of healthcare programs as they are lacking in healthcare with 24.9% where as Massachussets State has shown impressive results with least percent of lacks healthcare of 4.2% though Poverty is 11.5%.Also it is observed that Texas State donot really have a considerable household income which could be the factors for more percent of lacks healthcare

When the same plot is observed for Poverty to Smokers, Utah State has recorded a least percent of 9.7 smokers for 11.7 percent of Poverty where as West Virginia records a highest percentage(26.7) of smokers for Poverty of 18.3%

Also, the above plot shows that for Age median of ~35 Arkansan and West Virginia States have recorded highest percentages of obesity levels.

Overall conclusion for US census Data for the Year 2014 states that Texas has recorded highest percentage of Health Lacks which could be the due to lack of proper Health Insuace or due to low household Income. However Texas population needs to address their health lacks and try to get possible best solutions to improve their health factors.
